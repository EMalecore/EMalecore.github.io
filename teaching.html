PACE (Plant and Animal Conservation Ecology) Seminar

The PACE (Plant and Animal Conservation Ecology) Seminar is a collaboration between the Institute of Plant Sciences (represented by Eva Malecore) and the Institute of Ecology and Evolution (represented by Jean-Yves Humbert). The seminar is directed to master and PhD students (and everyone else interested is welcome to join). The aim of the seminar is to show talks on topics related to plant and animal conservation and conservation ecology, including speakers outside academia that are somehow involved in conservation, sustainability, biodiversity and public outreach.

My tasks: selecting and inviting speakers; leading the seminar.

Statistical Analyses with R

Internal course on Statistical Analyses with R for graduate students and researchers.

My tasks: preparing the lectures and exercises; give the lecture; assist the students with the exercises.
